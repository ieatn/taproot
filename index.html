Our Story

Rooted in the Heart of Columbia City

Established in July 2012, Taproot Café & Bar is owner-operated and proudly located in the heart of Downtown Columbia City. We showcase the very best the Pacific Northwest has to offer — from seasonal ingredients sourced close to home, to craft cocktails that celebrate local flavors.

What makes us unique is our ever-changing menu — crafted from beginning to end by our passionate team, focusing on incredible flavors and the nourishing aspects quality food provides. The local art on our walls ties the threads of the neighborhood together, making Taproot a true community gathering place.

Whether you're joining us for a hearty sandwich, a stellar salad, a fresh juice, or our most classy cocktails in Columbia City — there's always a place for you at Taproot.
